## SR-X mk1

**Impedance:** 16 Ohm  

**Release date:** 1970  
**Price at launch:** 18,000 yen  
**Diaphragm thickness:** 3.8 microns  
**Sensitivity:** 95dB/100Vr.ms  
**Weight:** 370g (excluding cord)  
**Diaphragm thickness:** 3.8um  
**Discontinued:** around 1972
